SongPane now on the App Store and Google Play

After having to jump through a number of hoops to get to this point, I am proud to say that SongPane is now available in both the iOS App Store and the Google Play store.

In case you haven’t heard about it before, SongPane is a tool that enables singers and musicians to more efficiently organize their song repertoire and put together set lists for use in live performances.

My wish is to make SongPane available on as many platforms as possible and having them published on the two largest mobile app ecosystems out there is definitely a huge step in that direction.

Taking into account the Firefox Marketplace which has had the app listed for quite a while already, as a SongPane user you now have access to the app in three different stores at no extra charge—all for your convenience:

  • SongPane on the iOS App Store for any iPad, iPhone and iPod touch with iOS 6 or newer.
  • SongPane on Google Play for devices running Android 4.4 KitKat or newer.
  • SongPane in the Firefox Marketplace for devices running on Firefox OS and any desktop operating system with the Firefox web browser installed.
