SongPane App

Manage set lists in a beautiful, efficient way.

Carefully crafted for use in live performances.

or take the demo for a spin.

  • Manage your song repertoire.

    Store all your songs in the cloud then put together sets for worship services, practice sessions or live performances. Say goodbye to that binder!

  • Enjoy rich portability.

    Your song catalog and set lists are always with you, on any device—desktop, tablet, or phone.

  • Share with band members.

    Give your mates access to any of your sets so you can all be on the same page.

  • Stay in sync.

    Benefit from real-time synchronization between all your devices and all of your friends’ devices.

  • Works offline.

    Your back is covered even when you are not connected to the internet.
